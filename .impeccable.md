## Design Context

### Users
Primary audience is mixed: academia, general readers, and potential collaborators.
The homepage hero must quickly orient first-time visitors and support collaboration intent, especially by making contact pathways feel natural and credible.

### Brand Personality
Calm, thoughtful, and authoritative.
The interface should communicate intellectual depth without visual noise, with a composed editorial rhythm over flashy effects.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined editorial layout with restrained, cinematic movement.
Motion should be minimal and intentional: staged reveals and subtle image drift that preserve readability and avoid performance-heavy theatrics.
Reduced-motion handling should keep only short, low-intensity opacity transitions.

### Design Principles
1. Lead with trust: the first screen should establish credibility in under three seconds.
2. Keep motion purposeful: animate meaningfully to guide attention, not to decorate.
3. Preserve calm hierarchy: typography and spacing carry structure before color or effects.
4. Support contact intent: interaction cues should nudge collaboration without aggressive CTAs.
5. Respect accessibility defaults: reduced-motion users receive a stable, readable experience.
